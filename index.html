Why We Built This

Sending crypto shouldn't feel like a bank transfer. We wanted to make it fun, personal, and unforgettable. Every gift on GIFT BOT is a unique NFT with custom artwork, traits, and a personal message — all stored forever on the blockchain.

How It Works

Connect your wallet, pick a gift style, add crypto and a message, then send it. The recipient gets a beautiful NFT they can claim anytime. No apps to install. No accounts to create. Just a link.
